Aegis Messenger Agreement

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

1. Secure Communication

Aegis Messenger provides End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) for all messages, calls, and media. This means only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to what is sent, and nobody in between, not even Aegis Messenger, can access your content.

2. Privacy by Design

We do not store your messages on our servers once they are delivered. Your messages are stored on your own device. We do not sell, rent, or monetize your personal data or messages in any way.

3. User Responsibility

You are responsible for keeping your device and your Aegis account safe and secure. You must notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account or any other breach of security.

4. Vishing Protection

Our AI-powered Vishing Protection is designed to help identify potential voice phishing attempts. While we strive for high accuracy, this feature is a tool to assist you and should not be solely relied upon for security decisions.

5. Data Usage

Aegis Messenger uses minimal metadata to facilitate communication. This includes your phone number (for account identification) and basic technical logs to ensure service reliability.
