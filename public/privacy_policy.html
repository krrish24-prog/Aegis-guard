Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Aegis Guard collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information needed to operate the app, such as account identifiers, display name, email address, profile photo, device details, contacts you choose to add, messages, reports, settings, feedback, and service logs.

2. Messages, Media, and Encryption

Aegis Guard is designed for secure communication. Message and media content may be encrypted or protected before storage or transmission depending on the feature. Some metadata, such as sender, recipient, timestamps, and delivery status, may be processed so the service can work.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide messaging, account access, contact discovery, media transfer, security analysis, abuse prevention, support, diagnostics, product improvement, and legal compliance.

4. AI and Safety Features

When you use AI safety analysis, relevant message or media details may be processed to detect suspicious content, phishing, malware indicators, or other risks. These features are intended to assist users and may not detect every threat.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with infrastructure providers, authentication services, storage providers, analytics or security tools, legal authorities when required, or other users as needed to deliver the service.

6. Storage and Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, protect users, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security. You may delete certain account, chat, or contact data from within the app where supported.

7. Your Choices

You can update profile information, adjust privacy settings, manage contacts, delete supported content, and contact us with privacy requests through the Contact & Feedback option in the app.

8. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards such as encryption, access controls, monitoring, and secure infrastructure practices. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Children

Aegis Guard is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law. Users who are not old enough to consent must use the service only with appropriate parental or guardian permission.

10. International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. We take steps designed to protect information when it is transferred or processed internationally.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted in the app or on this page with a new effective date.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, support, or feedback, use the Contact & Feedback option inside Aegis Guard.
