Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

This policy explains how Aegis Guard handles information inside the app.

1. Information We Use

We use information needed for the app to work, including your account identifier, display name, email, profile photo, contacts you choose to add, settings, messages, and security status.

2. Messages and Security

Aegis Guard is designed for secure communication. Messages, media, and chat data are used to deliver conversations, show chat history, and run safety checks requested by the app experience.

3. Profile and Contacts

Your profile photo, name, status, and contact details may be shown to people you chat with or add as contacts. You can update these details from Settings.

4. Safety Analysis

AI Guard features may analyze messages or files to detect phishing, suspicious links, malware indicators, or unsafe patterns. These results are shown to help you make safer decisions.

5. Your Controls

You can update your profile, manage contacts, delete supported chats, change privacy settings, and contact support through the Contact & Feedback option inside Aegis Guard.
